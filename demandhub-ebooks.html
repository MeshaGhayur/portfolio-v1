DemandHub Ebooks

These designs are a few pages from a set of 3 e-books I created for DemandHub, a software company. These books were created to visualize a few strategies online businesses can adopt to grow in the market. With a few text-heavy pages and statistical figures to deal with, I approached the design with a clean and colourful yet professional vibe. All 3 e-books slightly differ in page layouts to mantain uniqueness but are all connected with the consistent use of illustrated characters, bright colours and large images.

Tools: Illustrator | Photoshop | InDesign
