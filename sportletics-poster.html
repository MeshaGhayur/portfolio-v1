Sportletics Poster

As part of an internship at Sportletics Canada, I used Photoshop to design this informational flyer to promote their new fitness programs. The task at hand required an eye catching flyer that appeals to both students and parents. To accomodate for the text-heavy look, I used bright colours, pictures and fun shapes. Hierarchy played a crucial role in mantaining the overall readability for this flyer.
