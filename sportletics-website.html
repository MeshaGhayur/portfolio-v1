Sportletics Website

Tasked with a redesign for the brand's website, I worked with a team to design this multi-page responsive website for Sportletics Canada. The design focused on transforming Sportletics' current professional looking asthetic to a fun and cheery one that encourages school students to sign up for their fitness programs. I later used HTML, CSS and Bootstrap to convert the design into a functional website which includes carousels, large imagery and overlapping text-image modules that give the website a freidnly vibe.
